Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use the crop recommendation feature?

To use the crop recommendation feature, navigate to the "Crop Recommendation" section and select your region and soil type. Then click on "Get Recommendations" to view the suggested crops for your area.

How accurate are the disease predictions?

Our disease prediction system utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms trained on extensive datasets. While we strive for accuracy, please note that predictions may vary depending on various factors such as image quality and disease severity.

Can I request a new feature?

Absolutely! We welcome any feedback and feature requests from our users. Feel free to send us your suggestions via email or through our community forum.

How do I interpret fertilizer recommendations?

Our fertilizer recommendation system considers factors such as soil nutrient levels, crop type, and growth stage to suggest optimal fertilizer types and application rates. We provide detailed explanations along with recommendations to help you understand and apply them effectively.
