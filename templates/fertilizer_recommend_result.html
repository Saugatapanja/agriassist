10-26-26

This fertilizer contains 10% nitrogen (N), 26% phosphorus (P₂O₅), and 26% potassium (K₂O). It is a high-phosphorus fertilizer, suitable for promoting root development and flowering in plants. Use it before planting or during the early stages of growth to encourage strong root systems and robust flowering.

{%elif data == 1%}

14-35-14

Similar to the 10-26-26 fertilizer, this one is also high in phosphorus (14%) but with a higher concentration of potassium (35%). It's ideal for stimulating root growth, flower formation, and overall plant vigor. Apply it before planting or during the flowering stage to support healthy blooms and fruit production.

{%elif data == 2%}

17-17-17

This fertilizer contains equal parts of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, each at 17%. It provides a balanced nutrient ratio suitable for promoting overall plant growth and development. Use it during the early stages of growth or throughout the growing season to ensure plants receive essential nutrients for healthy foliage, root development, and fruit production.

{%elif data == 3%}

20-20

Similar to 17-17-17, this fertilizer has a balanced nutrient ratio with 20% nitrogen and 20% phosphorus. It's suitable for general-purpose applications across various stages of plant growth. Use it when you need a balanced nutrient supply to support overall plant health and productivity.

{%elif data == 4%}

28-28

This fertilizer is high in nitrogen (28%) and phosphorus (28%). It provides a significant boost of both nutrients, making it suitable for promoting rapid vegetative growth and early plant establishment. Use it during the vegetative stage or when plants need a strong growth stimulus.

{%elif data == 5%}

DAP

DAP is a granular fertilizer containing nitrogen (18%) and phosphorus (46%). It's a quick-release fertilizer suitable for providing an immediate nutrient supply to plants. Use DAP during planting or early growth stages to support root development and early plant vigor.

{%elif data == 6%}

Urea

Urea is a nitrogen-rich fertilizer containing approximately 46% nitrogen. It's a soluble fertilizer that provides a readily available nitrogen source for plants. Use urea during the growing season to boost nitrogen levels in the soil and promote lush foliage growth. Ensure proper application to prevent nitrogen loss through leaching or volatilization.
